About Me

I'm currently a freelance graphic designer studying to become a full-stack web developer. I graduated from California University of Pennsylvaniain 2012 with a Bachelor of Science focused on Graphic Design. Since then, I have been freelancing and doing contract work for an educational company that specializes in student evaluation assessments. While I do enjoy graphic design, I couldn't contain my interest in web development and therefore decided to expand my knowledge via the John Hopkins Coding Bootcamp.

If I'm not in front of my laptop, I'm probably hanging from a rock, as climbing is my favorite pastime and stress reliever. I also enjoy hiking, camping, traveling, video games, Dungeons & Dragons, and coffee...lots of coffee.
